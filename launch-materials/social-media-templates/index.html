    Response Templates

    Positive Comments

    Reply to "This is amazing!":

    Thank you! We're excited to help make services more accessible. If you know someone who could benefit, please share. 💙

    Reply to "I needed this!":

    We're so glad it helps! Let us know if you have any feedback or suggestions. We're always improving.

    Critical Feedback

    Reply to "This service info is wrong":

    Thank you for letting us know! Please send corrections to feedback@helpbridge.ca and we'll update it ASAP. Accuracy is our top priority.

    Reply to "Why isn't [service] listed?":

    Great question! We're gradually expanding. You can submit services for review at helpbridge.ca/submit-service or email feedback@helpbridge.ca

    Technical Questions

    Reply to "Does this work on iPhone?":

    Yes! Works on any device with a web browser (iPhone, Android, tablets, computers). You can also add it to your home screen like an app.

    Reply to "How do you protect privacy?":

    By default, searches happen entirely on your device—we never see your queries. No user accounts, no tracking, no logging. Privacy policy at helpbridge.ca/privacy

    Visual Asset Checklist

    Note: These need to be created by a designer. Specifications below.

    Required Graphics

    1. Open Graph Images (for social media previews)

    • Size: 1200×630px
    • Format: PNG or JPG
    • Content: Logo + tagline + URL
    • Use: Auto-preview when sharing links on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn

    2. Logo Variants

    • Full Color: On white background
    • White: For dark backgrounds
    • Icon Only: Square version for profile pictures
    • Formats: SVG (primary), PNG (fallback)

    3. Feature Highlight Graphics (5 graphics)

    • Privacy-first (lock icon)
    • Works offline (phone with wifi-off icon)
    • 7 languages (flags or "hello" in languages)
    • Crisis support (heart or helping hands icon)
    • Accessibility (accessibility symbol)

    Size: 1080×1080px (Instagram square) Style: Consistent color palette, clean typography

    4. Launch Announcement Graphic

    • Size: 1080×1080px (Instagram) + 1200×630px (Facebook)
    • Content: "Launching Now" + logo + key features + URL
    • Style: Bold, celebratory

    5. Story Templates (Instagram/Facebook Stories)

    • Size: 1080×1920px
    • Content: Quick tips, feature highlights, testimonials
    • Quantity: 5-10 reusable templates

    Color Palette

    Primary Colors:

    • Teal/Aqua: #4FD1C5 (trust, calm)
    • Dark Teal: #319795
    • White: #FFFFFF
    • Dark Gray: #2D3748

    Accent Colors:

    • Orange: #ED8936 (action, warmth)
    • Red: #F56565 (alerts, urgency for crisis features)

    Backgrounds:

    • Light mode: White/light gray gradient
    • Dark mode: Dark gray/black gradient

    Typography

    Headings: System fonts (Inter, SF Pro, Roboto) Body: Same as headings for consistency Style: Clean, accessible, high contrast

    Platform-Specific Best Practices

    Twitter/X

    • Optimal Post Time: 9am, 12pm, 3pm EST
    • Frequency: 1-3 tweets/day max
    • Tone: Direct, informative, empathetic
    • Engagement: Reply to mentions within 2 hours

    Facebook

    • Optimal Post Time: 1-4pm EST
    • Frequency: 3-5 posts/week
    • Tone: Conversational, community-focused
    • Engagement: Join relevant Kingston groups (with permission)

    LinkedIn

    • Optimal Post Time: 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm EST (professional hours)
    • Frequency: 2-3 posts/week
    • Tone: Professional, data-driven
    • Target: Social workers, case managers, service providers

    Instagram

    • Optimal Post Time: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm EST
    • Frequency: 4-5 posts/week + daily stories
    • Tone: Visual, emotional, community-focused
    • Features: Use Stories for quick tips, Reels for feature demos

    Don'ts - What NOT to Do

    ❌ Don't use fear-based marketing

    • "People are dying because they can't find help" ❌
    • "We help people find critical services" ✅

    ❌ Don't make unverifiable claims

    • "Best social services directory in Canada" ❌
    • "Find 196+ verified services in Kingston" ✅

    ❌ Don't share user data or stories without explicit permission

    • Testimonials require written consent
    • Anonymize all usage examples

    ❌ Don't engage with trolls or negativity

    • Respond professionally once
    • Block/mute if harassment continues
    • Don't get into arguments

    ❌ Don't over-promote

    • Focus on value and community
    • Share 80% helpful content, 20% promotion
    • Avoid sounding like an ad
